## 🚀 Overview
ExpressVPN for Windows is a virtual private network app that routes your internet traffic through a secure, encrypted tunnel so your online activity stays private. It hides your real IP address, protects your data on shared and public Wi-Fi, and lets you connect to servers in many locations around the world. So what is Express VPN in practice? It is a straightforward privacy tool for anyone who wants to keep their browsing to themselves — one click connects you, and your connection is encrypted from that moment on. After you install ExpressVPN, signing in to your account activates the app on your PC.

## ✨ Features
- **Strong encryption** — Secures your internet traffic with modern encryption so no one on your network can read what you send or receive.
- **IP address masking** — Replaces your real IP address with one from a server location of your choice for more private browsing.
- **Global server network** — Connect to servers in many countries to change your virtual location while you are online.
- **Public Wi-Fi protection** — Keeps your data safe on cafés, airports, and other shared networks where security is uncertain.
- **One-tap connect** — Start protection with a single click, then let the app pick a fast, reliable server for you.
